Portfolio

My portfolio showcases a diverse range of design projects, from corporate branding to digital art and web design. Each piece represents a unique challenge and an opportunity to solve problems visually. I specialize in creating clean, modern aesthetics with a focus on user experience and storytelling. Explore my work to see the process behind each design, including initial sketches, iterations, and final polished pieces.

About Me

I'm Kelsey Schaefermeyer, a passionate graphic designer with a love for visual storytelling. My journey into design started with a fascination for colors, typography, and digital media. Over the years, I've honed my skills in branding, user interface design, and digital illustration. My design philosophy is centered around simplicity, functionality, and emotion.
